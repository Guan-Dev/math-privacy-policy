Third-Party Advertising in Children's Apps

Our application includes **non-personalized advertising** provided by Google AdMob. We are committed to showing only ads that are suitable for children and that comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other relevant privacy laws.

Here's how we ensure compliance for advertising in our app:

  • We have explicitly configured our Google AdMob SDK to treat all ad requests as **child-directed content** by setting `tagForChildDirectedTreatment: ChildDirectedTreatment.yes`. This prevents the serving of interest-based ads (personalized ads) and remarketing.
  • We have set the maximum ad content rating to **'G' (General audiences)** by setting `maxAdContentRating: MaxAdContentRating.g`. This ensures that only ads suitable for all ages, including families and children, are displayed.
  • **No personal data, persistent identifiers, or device information that could be used to track individual children over time or across different services is collected or used for advertising purposes.** All advertising is contextual, meaning it is based on the content of the app itself, not on the user's behavior or personal information.
  • Google AdMob's privacy policies for child-directed apps can be found at:
    • Google Publisher Policies for Families (AdMob)
    • Tagging Your Ad Requests for Child-Directed Treatment (AdMob)
